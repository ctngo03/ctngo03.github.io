My Opinions on Lingerie in Day to Day Wear

How do you feel about the trend?

I see no harm in the trend. I think it is a fun and new way to approach the style of business casual. It also allows for the clothing to be worn in more places other than the privacy of your own home. I particularly love the way it is style over regular clothing, for instance, a lacey bra layered over a white shirt. I think it adds character to clothing that may be lacking in detail or color.

Would you try this trend?

Definitely! I've always loved the way lingerie has a dainty and feminine feel to it. Combining its soft features with the professional/clean aspects of regular clothing makes for a brilliant contrast.
